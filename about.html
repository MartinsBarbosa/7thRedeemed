About the 7th Redeemed

The seventh art - cinema - can inspire, educate, and bring families together. Yet for us, as Reformed Christians, finding movies that align with our values has become increasingly challenging. How many times have you started a film only to turn it off, disappointed?

Not anymore.

Introducing The 7th Redeemed - your trusted companion in discovering movies that honor God and uplift your spirit. Our script-by-script analysis helps you confidently choose entertainment that can be freely enjoyed without fear of offending our Lord and aggravating our faith in Jesus Christ.

Together, we're redeeming the seventh art, one movie at a time.
